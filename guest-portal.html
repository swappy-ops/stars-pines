The Ridge Guide

What you need to know while you're here. Not the tourist circuit — the actual ridge.

Places
Kasar Devi Temple
Go early, before 7 AM. The silence in the compound at dawn is different from anywhere else on the ridge.
0.1 km from the house
Chitai Temple (Gollu Devata)
Thousands of bells and handwritten wishes. Strange, beautiful, and deeply Kumaoni.
5 km
Almora Bazaar
Old hill-town bazaar with copper shops, wool sellers, and bal mithai better than anywhere else in Kumaon.
5 km
Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary
Dense oak forest, zero-light zone, panoramic Himalayan views from the rest house at the top.
35 km · Full day
Trails
